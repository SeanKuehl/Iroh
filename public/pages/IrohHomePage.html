What's Iroh?

Iroh is an online knowledge sharing community where all are welcome. Share and learn experiences in everything from coding to cooking. If you're looking for an open place to share and find knowledge, then Iroh is the right place!

How did Iroh come to be?

Iroh is founded and maintained by me, Sean. I'm a computer science student interested in web development and technologies. This interest lead me to develop Iroh, which hopefully can serve as an effective resume project as well as online community.
